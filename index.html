Why Blockchain?

Blockchain has the potential to touch every aspect of our daily lives by enabling individuals and companies across the globe to operate with a newfound level of trust and confidence.

At the present moment, however, most people know nothing about the technology aside from its association with Bitcoin. Beyond the lack of general awareness surrounding Blockchain, university students already interested in the subject have scarce educational and career opportunities.

BAF addresses the hurdles to blockchain mass adoption by inspiring and educating its next generation of leaders and users.

About Us

We are a network of students, professors, blockchain advocates and interested citizens who want to facilitate the mass adoption of blockchain technology. BAF was created by three recent college graduates looking to address the lack of opportunities for people interested in blockchain technology.
